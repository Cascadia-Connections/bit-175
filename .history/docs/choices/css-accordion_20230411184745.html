Expanding text

CSS

  • What is an accordion?

    An accordion is a kind of expanding text, used to show (and hide) HTML content. It often displays as a list of items stacked on top of one another. When clicked on (or triggered by a keyboard interaction or screen reader), these headers will either reveal or hide associated content.

  • What kind of content would I use this for?

    There are all kinds of uses. It's often used for an FAQ (frequently asked questions), a troubleshooting guide, or a glossary.

  • Why would I use this?

    It's often used to save space, to let people scan just the categories or questions, find the one they want, and then expand it to see the answer. They don't have to scroll through a bunch of content that's not pertinent to what they're trying to do.
